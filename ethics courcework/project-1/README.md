## üß† Project Structure & Workflow

1. **Understanding the Ethical Context**
   - Read and analyze the assigned academic paper
   - Identify ethical concerns, assumptions, and limitations

2. **Data Exploration**
   - Load and examine the provided dataset
   - Perform exploratory data analysis to understand patterns and constraints

3. **Analytical Reasoning**
   - Connect observed data behavior with claims or assumptions in the paper
   - Evaluate potential biases, limitations, and risks

4. **Ethical Evaluation**
   - Assess societal, fairness, and interpretability implications
   - Discuss responsible use of data and conclusions

5. **Communication**
   - Present findings clearly through a notebook, report, and HTML output
